About

Hello, I'm Teja Anand Pedapudi, from Machilipatnam, Andhra Pradesh. With a deep passion for embedded systems and electronics, I'm dedicated to crafting innovative hardware-integrated solutions that bridge the gap between the digital and physical worlds. My interests lie in real-time systems, IoT, TinyML, and microcontroller-based automation. As a motivated and disciplined engineering graduate, I bring not only strong technical skills in C, ARM, and embedded protocols, but also a collaborative mindset and a problem-solving attitude.
